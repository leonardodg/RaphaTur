City Tour in Florianópolis

Get to know Florianópolis - SC - Brazil

Florianópolis, is the capital city of the State of Santa Catarina,and is also known as "The Island of Magic". It is located on the east coast of Santa Catarina, It is composed of one main island, the Island of Santa Catarina (Ilha de Santa Catarina), surrounding small islands, as well as a continental part which was incorporated in 1927 along with the construction of the Hercílio Luz suspension bridge - 820 m long - that linked the island to the mainland. It is approximately between 20 to 40 meters high.

Florianópolis is one of the three island capitals of Brazil. It is becoming more and more established as a tourist center thanks to the many stunning beaches (Jurerê, Canasvieiras, Ingleses, Armação, among others) that surround the island and the beauty of Lagoa da Conceição, 13 km away from the city center. Some principal attractions of this area are the historical monuments of the city, the house of Vitor Meireles, the forts and the metropolitan cathedral, as well as the traditional Bobbin laces, of the Azorean people, which can be purchased from street-side venders.

Florianópolis is a privileged city when it comes to tourism with nautical, urban and mountain sports; cultural and architectural heritage; beaches; lagoons; hiking trails; boating, paragliding and helicopter tours; great restaurants, hotels and inns to name a few! The list of attractions in Florianópolis is inexhaustible!
