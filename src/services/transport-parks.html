Transport to Parks in Florianópolis

To better serve you, our team offers the a transport service to the best amusement parks located in our region. We offer recreational packages for your group of friends or for your whole family. This includes the Beto Carrero World Theme Park and the Aqua Show Water Park. Choose your adventure and enjoy yourself!



BETO CARRERO WORLD

Transportion to the largest theme park in Latin America, Beto Carrero World, for up to 14 people $400 round trip

Beto Carrero World is located in Penha, Santa Catarina. The park opened in 1991 and since then has been elected the best theme park in Brazil in both 2011 and 2012, as well as one of the top six theme parks in the world.



AQUASHOW WATER PARK

Transportation to the best water park in Santa Catarina, Agua Show Water Park, for up to 14 people $200 round trip

The AquaShow Water Park is located in the north island of Florianópolis, on highway SC 403, near Praia dos Ingleses, 30 km from the city center. The Aqua Show complex is the best water park in Santa Catarina. It houses several areas for entertainment and recreation, as well as restaurants, bars, and snack bars.
