Our Company

We welcome English speaking foreigners from around the world to Santa Catarina. Our headquarters is located in Florianópolis where we have been working in the transportation industry for the past 5 years. We provide private transport services to diverse locations, such as restaurants, tours, clubs, shows, hotels, as well as tourist information.

Enjoy transfers and personalized tours throughout the State of Santa Catarina, in our executive van, with the capacity to carry 14 guests, and specialized English service.

Our Services: City-Tour in Florianópolis, Tourist in Florianópolis, Receptive in Florianópolis, Transport to Parks in Florianópolis, Transport to Restaurants in Florianópolis, Transport to Nightclubs in Florianópolis, Rent Van in Florianópolis, Tansfer in Florianópolis, Tansport of Van to Florianópolis.

Mission

To provide quality tourism services to English speaking visitors. We attend all areas of the Florianopolis region. We contribute a lot of local knowledge, as well as focus on the customer’s experience, helping boost the entire tourism industry.

Vision

Being renowned internationally as the best shuttle service in Santa Catarina.

Values

We value ethics, professionalism and transparency in customer relations, in addition to professional development of our employees. We focus on results, always seeking to improve.
